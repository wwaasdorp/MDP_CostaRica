For non-commercial request of the camera calibration toolbox, send a request email to jean-yves.bouguet@intel.com

Please, mention clearly in your request email that you will not use the calibration toolbox for any commercial purpose. It is also very appreciated if a brief description of the project(s) the toolbox will be (potentially) used for is included in your request email (few words are enough: design of a stereo camera system for vehicle navigation, or student who wants to learn quickly about camera calibration, or student who wants to quickly get started on his machine vision project without having to learn and implement camera calibration).

This toolbox is for personal use only. If you would like to have it available to a group of people (such as a research group), then mention it clearly in your request email with the names (and possibly email addresses) of all the users, or simply the name of the laboratory interested (e.g. Caltech Computer Vision Lab, http://www.vision.caltech.edu).

Thank you very much,

- Jean-Yves Bouguet - Dec. 1999.
